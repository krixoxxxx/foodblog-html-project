Pancakes

  • The pancakes were the most fun, they looked really messy because of 1 pancake not having anything in it and the whipped cream to cover up the spilt powdered sugar. The texture of the pancakes was normal, the pancake on the top felt a bit mushy though.
  • The aroma was amazing because the mango added a nice fruit smell while the whipped cream radiated sweetness.
  • I learnt to plan ahead what I will put into pancakes.
  • I enjoyed making the pancakes a lot because they're so easy and simple.
  • Finding the right heat was a bit hard.
  • I would not change anything.
