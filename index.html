Chocolates

The chocolates' taste was a bit overpowering, the milk chocolate was very salty, the dark was very good but the white was overpowering. The aroma of the dark chocolate was rich, although the rest felt like ordinary chocolate.

I didn't learn any new skills.

I enjoyed pouring and mixing the chocolate, working with chocolate is rather calming.

A challenge I faced was not pouring too much chocolate into the mold, I spilt a tiny bit at one point.

If I were to modify the experiment, I would add a double boiler and more interesting ingredients.

Pancakes

The pancakes were the most fun. They looked really messy because of one pancake not having anything in it, and the whipped cream to cover up the spilt powdered sugar. The texture of the pancakes was normal, though the top pancake felt a bit mushy.

The aroma was amazing because the mango added a nice fruit smell while the whipped cream radiated sweetness.

I learnt to plan ahead for what I will put into pancakes.

I enjoyed making the pancakes a lot because they're so easy and simple.

Finding the right heat was a bit hard.

I would not change anything.

Burrito Bowl

The burrito bowl was a bit "meh." The rice got burnt, and the table got messy. However, the food tasted amazing! The beef was dry, but the mix of sour cream, salsa, rice, and beef was simply supreme.

The food had a rich aroma due to the beef, and the touch of lemon juice was great.

I learnt to use lower heat to reduce liquids better.

I enjoyed this because it was really delicious.

I burnt the rice. :C

I'd use a rice cooker next time—cooking in a pot was painful.

Milkshake

The milkshake was amazing! I had two of them. They were a little messy and runny, and the chocolate at the bottom didn’t blend, but they were still delicious.

The smell was strong with caramel and Oreo—a very sweet aroma.

I learnt that I should definitely blend for longer.

I enjoyed the milkshakes because they were simply amazing.

The milkshake wasn't fully blended.

I'd have more chocolate.

Rocky Road

The rocky road wasn’t as good—it was very thick and just mediocre.

Didn’t have much of an aroma except for a light Oreo.

I learnt to better plan chocolate-making.

I enjoyed the rocky road a bit, but it was too chunky to eat properly.

The rocky road didn’t solidify fast enough.

I'd use a bigger tray so the chocolate isn’t as chunky.

Soft Tacos

The tacos were a bit salty but very good—they were very messy but still amazing.

The aroma was very rich in umami because of all the seasonings and beef stock.

I didn’t learn anything new.

I enjoyed the tacos a lot—the mess was okay because everything was amazing.

The tacos wouldn’t stay together.

I'd add more meat so the tacos would be yummier.
